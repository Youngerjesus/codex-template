# Phase Gates

## Phase 1: Root Cause Investigation

Do not advance until you can explain:
- the observed symptom
- the expected behavior
- the most likely failing boundary or assumption
- the evidence that supports that claim

## Phase 2: Pattern Analysis

Do not advance until you compared the broken path with a working baseline when one exists.

## Phase 3: Hypothesis And Testing

Do not advance until you stated one hypothesis and ran one experiment that narrows the cause.

## Phase 4: Implementation

Do not call the work done until the reproduction passes and broader verification runs cleanly.
